# Creator Partnership Research

Use this workflow to build or compare a creator shortlist for a product or category.

## Workflow

1. Establish the product, region, target audience, and any follower, engagement, sales, or creator-size constraints.
2. Call `creator_search` with category and product relevance first, then performance and scale constraints.
3. Evaluate category fit, product history, available sales, GPM, average views, interaction rate, follower count, product count, and contactability.
4. Use `video_search` or `caption_extract_url` when content style and messaging fit are important to the decision.
5. Separate creators suitable for broad awareness, conversion-led testing, niche authority, or low-cost seeding.

## Deliverable

- Prioritized creator shortlist
- Fit and performance evidence for each recommendation
- Suggested product angle and collaboration format
- Risks such as weak category fit, overbroad product history, low engagement, or dependence on one viral video
- Recommended first outreach cohort and test allocation

Do not label creator sales as a specific recent period unless the returned field defines that period.

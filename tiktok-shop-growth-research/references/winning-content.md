# Winning Content Research

Use this workflow to find reference shoppable videos and convert them into reusable content insights.

## Workflow

1. Call `product_search` when needed to identify the correct product language, representative product IDs, and category boundaries.
2. Call `video_search` using the product keyword or IDs. Prefer a balanced sample across sales, views, engagement, recency, and creator size instead of ranking by one metric only.
3. Remove irrelevant or weakly related videos.
4. Call `caption_extract_url` on the most useful accessible videos.
5. Compare the videos across hook, problem/desire, demonstration, benefit, proof, objection handling, offer, CTA, pacing, and creator persona.

## Deliverable

- Ranked reference-video list with selection reasons
- The associated product and available performance fields
- Repeated content patterns versus one-off creative choices
- Hooks, selling points, proof, and CTA patterns grounded in source captions
- Elements safe to adapt and elements dependent on a particular creator or visual execution
- Three to five testable content briefs rather than copied scripts

State when a video relies mainly on visuals and caption data cannot explain its performance.
